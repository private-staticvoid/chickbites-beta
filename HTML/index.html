About Us

Welcome to ChickBites — One Bite Closer to Happiness. What started as a passion for fried chicken turned into a crave-worthy destination for comfort food lovers. At ChickBites, we serve up bold, sauced-up fried chicken alongside cheesy pastas, loaded sides, and ice-cold drinks to complete the experience.

 

We believe good food should be simple, satisfying, and unforgettable. Whether you're a fan of spicy kicks, garlic glazes, or sweet-savory Korean-style wings, there’s a flavor waiting for you here. Our menu is made with quality ingredients, cooked fresh, and served with heart.

 

Come hungry. Leave happy. That’s the ChickBites way.

Chickbites

At ChickBites, we're more than just a restaurant — we're a crew of flavor fanatics who believe that great food brings people together. Our team is made up of cooks, creatives, and friendly faces who put care into everything from seasoning the perfect batch of fried chicken to making sure your order hits the spot every time.

In the kitchen, our chefs are constantly refining our recipes to make sure every bite is bold, juicy, and unforgettable. Out front, our service team brings the energy, making sure every customer feels right at home — whether you're a first-time visitor or a regular who already knows your order by heart.

We may come from different backgrounds, but we’re united by a shared love for food that’s made right and served with heart. At ChickBites, we work as one to serve you not just a meal, but a moment worth craving.

At ChickBites, we believe great flavor starts with good ingredients and even better preparation. We use high-quality frozen chicken that’s carefully stored and cooked fresh to order, locking in flavor and tenderness every time.
